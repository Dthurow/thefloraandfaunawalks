Rubus parviflorus, common name Thimbleberries are some of my favorite wild berries out here! They have lots of seeds, so rather than crunching down on a berry, try mashing it with your tongue. Saves you seeds getting permanently stuck in your teeth üòÖ They're super tasty and quite soft when ripe, and thimbleberry jam is delicious!
